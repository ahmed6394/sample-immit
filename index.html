Stay safe with
CovPass App

CovPass is an app for residents of Germany. It lets them load an EU Digital COVID Certificate for their Covid-19 vaccination onto their smartphone. They can then show it as needed. What’s more, the app can also be used to manage certificates for recovery from a Covid-19 infection. The app is a free service provided by the Robert Koch Institute. Using it is voluntary.
